Presenting information

  • Basic principle: separate content and presentation
  • Presentation can have some special meaning:
    • different perceptions of the message
    • different cultures can give different meaning to the colors
    • different devices
    • different abilities

Multimedia, mathematics, graphics are often essential elements in the message, and are semantically significant:

  • SMIL supports rich multimedia presentations
  • MathML allows coding of mathematical formulae (every component can be individually identified, annotated, origin of links, etc.)
  • SVG is Scalable Vector Graphics, with elements that can be individually identified, annotated, origin of links, etc.
