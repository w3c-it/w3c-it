Multimedia: SMIL

  • SMIL (Synchronized Multimedia Integration Language) is the W3C Recommendation
    • interactive audiovisual presentations
    • integrates audio and video streaming with images, texts and other media
    • just use text editors
    • is a XML application
    • components identified by URI
    • designer specifies what and when show
