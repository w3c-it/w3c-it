Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG)

Major problems/challenges with graphics on the Web:

  • Large Download Times
  • No Separation of Style and Content
  • No Hyperlinking
  • No Metadata
  • Differences in the Range of Devices
  • Differences in the Range of Cultures
  • Differences in the Range of Users: Children to Great-Grandparents

SVG is an XML based graphics specification handling those issues
