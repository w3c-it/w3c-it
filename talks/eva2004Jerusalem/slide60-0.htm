Statements about statements

To affirm that a statement is reliable, we have to formulate statements about statements.

For example: Dov Winer says that resource http://www.w3c.it/Oreste/DocX (Paper) has Creator O. Signore .

In the Knowledge Representation community this process is known as "reification"
