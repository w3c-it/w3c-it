A simple example

Two equivalent expressions in natural language:

  • Oreste Signore is the author of DocX
  • The author of DocX is Oreste Signore

In RDF jargon:

Resource http://www.w3c.it/Oreste/DocX
Property author
Value Oreste Signore

With the following graphical representation:

Oreste Signore, Manager of W3C Office in Italy
