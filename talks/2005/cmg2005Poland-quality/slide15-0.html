The process

  • A possibly automated process for quality evaluation, using pages and and their components as elements to evaluate
  • Aimed to relate internal and external aspects
  • Identification of potential weakness points
  • Subsequent focused analysis (reduced costs, increased coverage)
  • In depth analysis of source code (including CSS)
  • Define a model of sites
  • A quality database stores results
  • Expert supplies additional (semantic) info
