Some considerations

  • Mainly qualitative criteria (errors, different evaluation)
  • Generalised criteria (they have to be weighted according to site characteristics)
  • Non orthogonal criteria (same characteristics accounted more than once)
  • Criteria are essentially accessibility or usability biased
  • Granularity (page or site)
  • External vs internal quality
  • A rigorous approach requires that characteristis are measurable:
    "You cannot control what you cannot measure" --- Tom De Marco
