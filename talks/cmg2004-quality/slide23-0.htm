Some readability indices

Readability is not just a recent and web related issue:
Flesh, and Rudolph, "The Art of Readable Writing", Macmillan Publishing, 1949

  • Flesch-Vacca (Italian version)
    206-(0,6*S) – P
    S = syllables/words
    P = average number of words per sentence
  • Gulpease (Gruppo Universitario Linguistico Pedagogico)
    89 – (Lp/10)+(3*Fr)
    Lp = (100 * total of characters)/total of words
    Fr = (100 * total of sentences)/total of words
  • Kinkaid (Italian version)
    11,8*S+0.39*P – 15,59
    S = sillables/words
    P = words/sentences
