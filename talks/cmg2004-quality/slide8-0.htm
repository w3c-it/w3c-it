Flexible approach to evaluate a generic web site

  • Mich L., Franch M., Gaio L., "Evaluating and Designing the Quality of Web Sites", IEEE Multimedia, Jan-Mar, 2003
  • A conceptual model to evaluate evaluate a generic web site quality
  • Takes its name from the initials of the Ciceronian loci on which it is based
Quis (Identity)
Who (brand, design, etc.)
Quid (Content)
What (coverage, accuracy)
Cur (Services)
Why (Functionalities, control)
Ubi (Location)
Where (Reachability, intuitive URL, interactivity, etc.)
Quando (Management)
When (Currentness, Maintenance)
Quomodo (Usability)
How (Accessibility, Navigability, Understandability)
Quibus auxiliis (Feasibility)
(Resources, Technology)
