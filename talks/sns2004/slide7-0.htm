The query

Frustrating traditional approaches:

  • select field from a pop up window
  • insert parentheses and Boolean operators (selecting from different options)
  • difficult to formulate complex queries
  • difficult to reuse elementary conditions

Composition:

  • A composition of several queryFragments (conceptually a single navigation upon the document tree)
  • A queryFragment is made by several queryTokens
  • A queryToken is the elementary component (a single interaction upon the queryForm on a single element)

Normalization:

  • identify the common ancestor for clauses on elements belonging to independent paths
