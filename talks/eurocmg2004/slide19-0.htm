Web accessibility: how much will it cost?

Some frequent sentences:

  • an accessible site is costing much more
  • an accessible site imposes limitation to the designer creativity
  • an appealing site can't take into account accessibility requirements

Some considerations:

  • WWW means World Wide Web
  • Consistency with international framework is a must
  • Design for quality, hence:
    • avoid useless technological gadgets
    • separate content, structure and presentation of the message
    • correctness
    • understandability
    • usability
  • The cost is in the acquisition of skills, but:
    • education is a must in a continuousloy evolving environment
    • quality is an investment, and is a key for reduced costs in maintenance
