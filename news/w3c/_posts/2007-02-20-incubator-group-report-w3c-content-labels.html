--- title: "Incubator Group Report: W3C Content Labels" date: 2007-02-20 categories: --- The W3C Content Label Incubator Group (WCL XG) published its final report, W3C Content Labels. The Incubator Report defines a data model for Content Labels through which identified parties can make assertions about the properties of a resource, or groups of resources. This publication is part of the W3C experimental Incubator Activity (see February 2006 press release) and is the first Incubator Group Report published by W3C. (Archivio News inglese, italiano)
