--- title: "XHTML 1.1 Second Edition: Working Draft" date: 2007-02-16 categories: --- The HTML Working Group released the second edition of XHTML 1.1 - Module-based XHTML as a Working Draft in preparation for Proposed Edited Recommendation. XHTML 1.1 is a reformulation of XHTML 1.0 Strict based on XHTML modules. Not a new version, the second edition incorporates all known corrections and adds a new description in XML schemas.
