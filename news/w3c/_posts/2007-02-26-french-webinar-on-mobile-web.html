--- title: "French Webinar on Mobile Web" date: 2007-02-26 layout: page categories: --- Join us for a free W3C Webinar in French, where you will learn how to make your Web content mobile-friendly. Cédric Kiss (W3C) will present the work of the Mobile Web Best Practices Working Group, including best practices, techniques, the mobileOK mark and a demonstration of the best practices checker. The Webinar will be held on Tuesday 27 February at 14:00 UTC. Attendance is free but registration is required. Visit the Mobile Web Initiative home page. (Archivio News inglese, italiano)
