--- title: "Distributed Web Applications: Advance Notice of Workshop" date: 2007-02-16 layout: page categories: --- W3C plans a Workshop on Declarative Models of Distributed Web Applications on 5-6 June 2007, hosted by MobileAware with the support of the Irish State Development Agency, Enterprise Ireland. Attendees will discuss application modeling, security and usability for distributed applications running on network devices. A Call for Participation and more information is expected in March.
