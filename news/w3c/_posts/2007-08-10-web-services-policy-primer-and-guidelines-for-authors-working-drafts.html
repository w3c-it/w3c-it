--- title: "Web Services Policy Primer and Guidelines for Authors: Working Drafts" date: 2007-08-10 categories: --- Il Web Services Policy Working Group ha rilasciato due Working Drafts aggiornati. Primer introduce il policy language ed i meccanismi policy attachment. Guidelines for Policy Assertion Authors fornisce buone prassi per creare policy assertion.
